Just for the record, with Docker 1.8 and docker-compose 1.4 there is already exists a method to limit log size using
https://docs.docker.com/compose/yml/#log-driver
and log-opt max-size:

  log_driver: "json-file"
  log_opt:
    max-size: "100k"
    max-file: "20"
