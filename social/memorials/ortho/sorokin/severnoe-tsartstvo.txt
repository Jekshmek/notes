Prosushestvovalo okolo 200 let (do zavoevaniya Asiriyskoi imperiei)

Posle smerti Solomona Ierovoam (creatura prorocheskih krugov) otpravlyaetsya nazad.
V Israile Solomonov prestol nasleduet ego syn Rovoam, kotory predprinyal oprometchivye shagi ... k raspadu strany.

Ierovoam vo glave delegacii severnyh plemen prosit nalogovyh poslableniy dlya severnyh plemen
Krug staryh Solomonovyh sovetnikov sovetuyut dat poslablenie
Krug novyh sovetnikov - proyavit zhestkost

Severnoe (samariyskoe) tsarstvo zanimaet vsyu Galileyu, dolinu Iordana, severnuyu chast Samarii
Ono - krupnee yuzhnogo po teritorii.
Naselenie - neodnorodno (evreev esli i bolshe, chem yazychnikov, to nenamnogo)
Economicheski - bolee razbito chem yug
Na severe nikogda ne bylo ustoichivoy tsarskoi vlasti
1-y tsar - Ierovoam, zatem - dvortsovye perevoroty

Severnomu tsarstvu - prihoditsya mnogo voevat: moavityane, siriya (ne putat s assiriytsami, u kotoryh poroi dazhe prosyat pomoshi),
    ne slishkom mirnye otnosheniya s finikiei
Yuzhanam - net (s filistimlyanami - pokoncheno)

Na severe nachinaetsya imuschestvennoe rassloenie.
Plemennye zemli - okazyvayutsya v rukah chuzhih lyudei.
Ne vse mogli nayti rabotu v gorodah (Iudeya - bolee stabilnaya patriarhalnaya selhoz-strana)
